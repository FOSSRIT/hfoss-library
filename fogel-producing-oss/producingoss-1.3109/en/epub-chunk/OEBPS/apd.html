Appendix D. Obsolete Appendix: (was: Why Should I Care What Color the Bikeshed Is?)

It is an eloquent disquisition on what tends to go wrong in group discussions, but to save space I no longer include it as an appendix in this book. You can view the entire post at bikeshed.org.
